Nerdiness quiz Competition is being conducted by PCC club, under Innovac 21. This contest will test your subject knowledge.

Participate and outsmart the other participants to reach at the peak of the Leaderboard. May the best nerd win!

  • This will be a 15 minute quiz contest.

  • This will be a points based competetion. Every participant has to complete the contest in 20 minutes. You have only one attempt at the quiz.

  • The rank will be decided according to the maximum points scored. The time taken to complete quiz will be used to break a tie.

  • It is an individual event.
  • Each participant can register only once.
  • Doubts regarding anything should be asked directly to the event coordinators only. Posting it in public forums will not be entertained.
  • Unfair means to guarantee advantage will lead to disqualification.
  • The answers should not be made public or open-sourced during the contest.
  • The decision of the organizers will be final and binding in any case of disputes.

Judging Criteria
At the end of the contest, the participants having highest rank on the leaderboard win.
