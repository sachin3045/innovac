Marathon Coding Competition is being conducted by PCC club, under Innovac 21. This contest will check your problem solving skill as well as evaluate how fast can you solve all the problems.

Participate and outsmart the other participants to reach at the peak of the Leaderboard. May the best coder win!

    This will be a 4 hour Coding contest, hosted at Hackerearth
  • This is an eliminative coding competition. Every 10 minutes or so, participant at the bottom of the leaderboard will be eliminated. (He/She can not make any more submissions).

  • The contest will have a winner when either only one participant remains, or participant at the top of the leaderboard at the end of the contest.

  • First elimination wave will take place 15 minutes after contest starting. In that, every participant that has 0 score will be eliminated.

  • After that, There will be an eliminative wave every 10 minutes, eliminating the last participant in the leaderboard.

  • It is an individual event.
  • If more than one participant have same score at the bottom of leaderboard, they will be eliminated in further waves.
  • Initially, there will be 7 questions. If one or more participants solve all the questions, new questions will be added.
  • Doubts regarding anything should be asked directly to the event coordinators only. Posting it in public forums will not be entertained.
  • Unfair means to guarantee advantage will lead to disqualification.
  • The solutions should not be made public or open-sourced during the contest.
  • The decision of the organizers will be final and binding in any case of disputes.

Judging Criteria
At the end of the contest, the participant having highest score on the leaderboard wins.
