Mind Matrix is being conducted by PCC club, under Innovac 21. This contest will check your decoding skills as well as evaluate how good you are at hacking.

Participate and outsmart the other participants to reach at the peak of the Hackerboard.

    This will be a 2 hour Hacking contest, hosted on our website Innovac.

  • There are three domains: Riddles, Picture decoding and web exploitation.

  • You to crack the first problem to reach on the next problem.

  • Points will be awarded for correct answers and hints will also be given after a definite number of attempts.

  • Points awarded to you will be shown on hackerboard.

  • It is an individual event.
  • There will be 5 questions in each domain. You can switch to next question only if you have attempted previous one.
  • Doubts regarding anything should be asked directly to the event coordinators only. Posting it in public forums will not be entertained.
  • Unfair means to guarantee advantage will lead to disqualification.
  • The solutions should not be made public or open-sourced during the contest.
  • The decision of the organizers will be final and binding in any case of disputes.

Judging Criteria
At the end of the contest, the participant having highest score on the hackerboard wins.
